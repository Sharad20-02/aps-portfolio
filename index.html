Design

The design of this portfolio focuses on simplicity and clarity, with organized sections that provide insights into various aspects of algorithmic problem-solving in Spotify.

Challenges

  • Search based on song, artist, albums or playlists.
  • Filtering song searches based on year of release, genre or artist.
  • Add and remove songs to queue.
  • Add and remove songs to playlists.
  • Sorting artists or albums or song titles.
  • Shuffle songs in playlist while playing music from a playlist.
  • Implement phonetic searches - Searches where even if the spelling for search are wrong, related content must be displayed.
  • Manage concurrent access to shared playlists.
  • Network of users, artists and their relationships. Also adding new friends / followers to the network.
  • Playback resumption from last position when users switch devices or re-open the app.
